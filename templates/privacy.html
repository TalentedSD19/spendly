Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect your name, email address, and password. As you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you add. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power your personal expense tracking experience. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access. Passwords are stored as secure hashes and are never stored in plain text.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with any third-party services. We use Google Fonts for typography, which may log your browser's request to their servers — this is standard web font loading behaviour and does not include any of your Spendly data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. You may also request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
